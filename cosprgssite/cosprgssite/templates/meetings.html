Quaker Meetings

Meeting for Worship

Quakerism offers a spirituality based on experiencing a loving inward presence, or Inner Light, which opens us to truth, freedom and peace. Our weekly Meeting for Worship is at 10 am each First Day (Sunday). In our Meeting for Worship, we gather together in prayerful silence for one hour, opening ourselves to this experience. All are welcome.

Business Meeting

We also have a monthly Business Meeting after worship on the third First Day of each month, where consensus decisions are made concerning the Meeting's financial, spiritual, and physical matters.
